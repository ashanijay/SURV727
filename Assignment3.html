Plot the common words with each location.

See the plots below. The most common words for each town are the names of the town itself and often the city of “Chicago” is mentioned. Some of the differences in the most common words are: Grand Boulevard, Chicago has words that describe the people that live in the “community” using words like “residents” and “american.” Hyde Park, Chicago has words that describe nature including “lake” and academia using the word “university.” Kenwood, Chicago has words that desribe the community and schooling. It also has the names of some of the neighboring towns. Oakland, Chicago has words that desribe the building and homes in the town. The word “lake” is also mentioned like in Hyde Park.
